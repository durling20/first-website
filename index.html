Biden

The Incumbent President

Coming in at 81 years old for reelection, we have our current President: Joe Biden. He has been criticized for his old age and is currently coming in behind his opponent in the polls. He wants to grow the middle class and create policies that benefit working families. At the recent debate he was slow and extremely hard to follow only making his chances worse.

Trump

The Former President

Coming in at 78 years old for reelection, we have our former President: Donald Trump. He has been convicted as a felon, unsuccessfully indicted twice, and has multiple misdemeanor accounts to his name. He is critisized mainly on those accounts. His campaign promises revolve around protecting the border, gun rights, and upholding pro-life/anti-choice policy. At the recent debate he spouted many demonstrably false claims. However, he said them confidently and clearly making him the winner of the debate.
