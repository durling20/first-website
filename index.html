Poem Title

This poem is inspired by my love for a game called Magic the Gathering. It can be a frustrating game, but I always want to play more.

I play my first spell

My opponent counters it

I will lose this game

My personal interests

I would like to expound on this game Magic the Gathering. This is a popular trading card game that revolves around deck building and casting spells to try and win. You can win in various ways, but the most common is to reduce your opponents life total to zero.

I also enjoy playing Mario Kart. I have grown up playing this game and I think I am very good. I am sure there are better players than me, but in groups I play with, I generally come out in first. It is a nastolgic and simply great game.

Another interest I have is politics. My family always manages to get into a good discussion about politics when we are together. I like hearing different perspectives and learning from people who see things different from me.
